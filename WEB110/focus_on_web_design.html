Focus on Web Design

  • What is the company/organization? Bethesda Softworks
  • What is the URL? Bethesda News
  • What is the purpose of the site? To keep people updated on the things they're doing or working on.
  • Who is the intended audience? People that play video games and specifically enjoy Bethesda games.
  • Do you believe the site reaches its audience? I do believe so. It's easy to navigate, the home page lists articles sorted by newest to oldist, and you can also search for what you're looking for.
  • Was the site useful to you? Why or why not? Yes it is. I use this site frequently when I'm waiting on an update or I heard a rumor and want to check.
  • List the colors and/or graphics that are used on the home page of this website, etc: The header and footer are a very dark grey or black and the background is a light grey. The image shown at the top of the page is blue that shows a few characters from some of their games.
